## Niche Thesis

- **Decision-driver:** Walk-in tub is a specific $3–10K product install; buyers are researching both product AND installer
- **TAM:** ~$500M/yr US walk-in tub market, 8–10% CAGR
- **Tailwind:** Same aging-in-place wave as AccessRemodel
- **Lead value:** $75–500 (shared/exclusive leads) + **5–10% brand affiliate ($200–500/sale)**
- **AI-search resilience:** Local + product-specific ("walk-in tub installer [city]") survives LLM aggregation
- **Data reuse:** ~50% of AccessRemodel contractor base also installs walk-in tubs — **cost savings on Outscraper**

## Monetization Angle (Differentiator)

WalkInTubPros leans heavier on **brand affiliate** than paid listings:

| Brand | Program | Commission |
|---|---|---|
| Kohler | Affiliate direct | $200/install |
| Safe Step Walk-In Tub | Affiliate | $300/install |
| Ella's Bubbles | Affiliate | 5% of install |
| American Standard | — | Lead sale only |

Install volumes in the US are ~50K/yr; capturing 0.5% = 250 sales × $300 avg = $75K/yr at maturity.

## Filter Schema

- Brands installed (Kohler, Safe Step, Ella's Bubbles, American Standard, Jacuzzi, Ariel)
- Installation time (1 day / 2 day / 3+ day)
- Hydrotherapy features (air jets, water jets, chromotherapy, heated seat)
- Wheelchair-accessible models available
- Warranty length offered
- Financing options (some brands offer 0% APR)
- Free in-home consultation
