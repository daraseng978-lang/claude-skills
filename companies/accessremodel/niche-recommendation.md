## Why ADA Bathroom Contractors Wins

1. **Binary decision-driver** — ADA compliance is a hard filter. Competitors who aren't ADA-specialized cannot fake it. This is the "dementia example" from the transcript applied to a larger adjacent market.

2. **Aging-in-place megatrend** — ~10K Americans turn 65 daily. Bathroom retrofit is the #1 aging-in-place remodel (per AARP). Demand is structural, not cyclical.

3. **High ticket, high lead value** — Remodels range $5K–$50K. Contractor CAC tolerance = $50–200/lead. Senior Living Apex (dementia) beats us on $/lead but competition and regulatory YMYL ranking friction is higher.

4. **Low niche competition** — Angie's List, Home Guide, HomeAdvisor are horizontal. None rank well for "ADA bathroom contractor [city]" long-tail. Gap is real per Ahrefs spot-check.

5. **AI-search resilient** — Queries like _"ADA-accessible bathroom contractor in [city] with roll-in shower and grab-bar installation"_ are specific, local, and decision-heavy. LLMs must cite sources.

6. **Expansion path** — Clean vertical ladder: bathroom → walk-in tubs → stair lifts → whole-home accessibility audits → aging-in-place certifications (CAPS directory).

## Why Not The Others

- **Luxury restroom trailers** — Frey's own directory already lives in this space. Red ocean vs. his SEO head start.
- **Dementia senior living** — Strong, but YMYL (Your Money Your Life) ranking friction + sensitive content review = slower launch. Keep as Phase 2 candidate.
- **Tap water / air quality** — Affiliate-only monetization caps revenue ceiling. Andy already dominating tap water.
- **Events** — Frey himself called the vertical "still sucks" — low per-lead value, fragmented supply.

## Recommendation

Proceed to Phase 0 execution with **ADA Bathroom Contractors** as the flagship property. Company brand **AccessRemodel** is chosen for expansion headroom (not just bathrooms).

Domain target: `accessremodel.com` (or `.co` fallback). Founder: availability check + purchase at Budget Gate #2.
