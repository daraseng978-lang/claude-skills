# Revenue Model — AccessRemodel

_CFO memo. Assumptions stated explicitly. Updated at each budget gate._

## 5 Revenue Streams

| # | Stream | Goes Live | Year 1 Contribution (midpoint) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Paid featured listings | Month 3 | **~60%** |
| 2 | Lead sales | Month 3 | ~20% |
| 3 | Amazon + brand affiliate | Month 2 | ~10% |
| 4 | Display ads (Mediavine) | Month 6–9 | ~10% |
| 5 | Micro-SaaS for contractors | Year 2 | 0% in Y1 |

### 1. Paid Featured Listings (primary)

| Tier | Price | Value prop |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Basic listing |
| Basic | $49/mo | Top-3 placement per city, photos, tags |
| Pro | $99/mo | + lead priority + CAPS badge verification |
| Agency | $249/mo | Multi-city + API + monthly lead reports |

Blended avg: **$75/mo**. Conversion: 0.5–1% of listed contractors via outbound over 60 days.

### 2. Lead Sales

- Shared lead (3 contractors): $30–50
- Exclusive lead: $75–150
- Enterprise package (walk-in tub OEMs): $200+

### 3. Affiliate

- Amazon Associates: 3% on home goods, AOV ~$80
- Direct brand affiliate (Kohler, Safe Step, Bathease): 5–10% per sale, $200–500 payouts

### 4. Display Ads

- Mediavine eligibility: 50K sessions/mo
- Home niche RPM: $15–30
- Skip sub-premium ad networks to protect UX + SEO

### 5. Micro-SaaS (Year 2)

Quote-building tool + CAPS compliance checklist, $29–79/mo. Ecosystem lock-in.

## Revenue Kill Criteria

Rotate the niche if:
- Day 90: <$200 MRR **and** <1K monthly visits
- Day 180: <$1K MRR **and** <5K monthly visits
- Any month where LTV:CAC drops below 10× (economics broken)
