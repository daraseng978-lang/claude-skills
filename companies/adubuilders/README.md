## Niche Thesis

- **Decision-driver:** ADU zoning rules vary dramatically by state/city; contractor must navigate permits, setbacks, utility hookups
- **TAM:** California alone permitted **28,000 ADUs in 2024** (up from 1K in 2016). Nationwide market ~$15B/yr and growing 25%+ CAGR
- **Tailwind:** Zoning reform — CA SB 9, WA HB 1337, OR HB 2001, NY ADU Act, many cities upzoning
- **Lead value:** $50–250 (projects $50K–$250K, contractor CAC tolerance high)
- **AI-search resilience:** Local + permit-specific ("ADU builder [city] permit") — highly local, decision-stage
- **No dominant incumbent:** BuildingAnADU.com, MaxableSpace.com exist but neither dominates search

## Unique SEO Play

State-by-state + city-by-city permit guides — massive long-tail opportunity. Each of the ~200 US cities that have enacted ADU reform deserves its own "ADU permit guide for [city]" page. This is the **programmatic SEO moonshot** of the portfolio.

Example page templates:
- `/california/los-angeles/adu-permit-guide`
- `/california/los-angeles/junior-adu-rules`
- `/washington/seattle/detached-adu-setbacks`
- `/oregon/portland/adu-tax-assessment`

With the right template: ~5,000+ programmatic pages from Day 1.

## Filter Schema

- ADU types built: detached, attached, garage conversion, junior ADU, basement
- Prefab vs site-built
- Permit navigation service (yes/no — huge value-add)
- Price per sq ft (range)
- Financing partners (Renofi, HomeTap, HELOC coordination)
- Design-build vs build-only
- Utility hookup experience
- Years in business
- Timeline average (months from permit to CO)
