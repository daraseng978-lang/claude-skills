## The 5 Selected

### #1 AccessRemodel — ADA Bathroom Contractors
- **Lead value:** $50–200
- **Moat:** ADA compliance is binary; non-specialists can't fake it
- **Tailwind:** Aging-in-place megatrend (~10K Americans turn 65 daily)
- **Expansion:** Whole-home accessibility → stair lifts → audits
- **Folder:** `accessremodel/`

### #2 DementiaCare — Memory Care Facilities
- **Lead value:** $500–2,000 (highest in portfolio)
- **Moat:** Dementia-specific care capability is a hard filter; APlaceForMom is generic
- **Tailwind:** 6.7M Americans with Alzheimer's, growing 7%/yr
- **Risk:** YMYL (Your Money Your Life) ranking friction — need authoritative content
- **Folder:** `dementiacare/`

### #3 WalkInTubPros — Walk-in Tub Installers
- **Lead value:** $75–500 (shared lead) + 5–10% brand affiliate on $3–10K installs
- **Moat:** Product-specific intent; different buyer than whole-bathroom remodel
- **Data reuse:** ~50% overlap with AccessRemodel contractor base
- **Direct affiliate partners:** Kohler, Safe Step, Ella's Bubbles — $200–500/sale commissions
- **Folder:** `walkintubpros/`

### #4 IVFCost — Fertility Clinic Price Transparency
- **Lead value:** $100–400
- **Moat:** Price transparency in IVF (where cost varies $12K–$30K per cycle) barely exists today — this is Frey's price-transparency thesis applied to a $7B market
- **Tailwind:** IVF demand growing 4–6%/yr; 1 in 6 couples affected
- **Unique angle:** Cost calculator + success rate overlay (public CDC data)
- **Risk:** Some YMYL friction; mitigate with evidence-based content + clinic-provided pricing
- **Folder:** `ivfcost/`

### #5 ADUBuilders — Accessory Dwelling Unit Contractors
- **Lead value:** $50–250 (remodels $50–200K)
- **Moat:** Zoning reform tailwind (CA SB 9, WA HB 1337, NY etc.) creating brand-new demand; no dominant directory yet
- **Tailwind:** California alone permitted 28K ADUs in 2024, up from 1K in 2016
- **Expansion:** State-by-state permit guides (huge long-tail SEO play)
- **Folder:** `adubuilders/`

## The 5 Rejected (and why)

**#6 CAPS Aging-in-Place Contractors** — Cannibalizes AccessRemodel's audience. Better as a sub-filter within AccessRemodel than a separate property.

**#7 Stair Lift / Mobility Ramp Installers** — Combined with ADA bathroom + walk-in tubs, this would over-concentrate portfolio in aging-in-place. Phase 2 candidate if one of the selected flops.

**#8 Bariatric Surgery Clinics** — High $/lead but medical tourism creates international complexity + regulatory disclosure requirements. Phase 2.

**#9 Luxury Restroom Trailers** — Red ocean. Frey is aware, experienced, and running a tight ship. Not worth the fight when other niches are open.

**#10 Tap Water Quality** — Andy's incumbent lead + pure affiliate ceiling makes it the lowest-upside choice of the ten.
