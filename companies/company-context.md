# Company Context — Directory Portfolio Co.

_Portfolio-level context. Each property has its own `README.md` with niche-specific details._

## Company

- **Nature:** AI-operated holding company for niche directory properties
- **Legal status:** TBD (founder to incorporate at Phase 1 if portfolio validates)
- **Founded:** 2026-04-19
- **Stage:** Phase 0 — 5-property parallel MVP

## Mission

Operate a portfolio of niche directories that help consumers make high-stakes decisions where price/feature transparency is weak. Monetize via paid listings, lead sales, affiliate, display ads.

| Property | Niche | Primary monetization |

| AccessRemodel | ADA bathroom contractors | Paid listings + lead sales |

## Founder Profile

- **Role:** Founder / Investor only
- **Time commitment:** 10–20 min/week (budget approvals only)
- **Decision rights:** Budget approval (single lever). Everything else delegated.
- **Risk tolerance:** Portfolio approach — expect 2–3 winners out of 5, kill the rest fast

## Thesis

1. **Directories are distribution-first** — programmatic SEO + topical authority = cheap traffic
2. **Data is the moat** — Crawl4AI + Claude automate formerly-manual enrichment
3. **AI-search resilient** — narrow niches with compliance/decision-driver filters survive LLM search
4. **Portfolio > moonshot** — marginal cost per property is ~$130 after shared fixed costs; 5 shots on goal
5. **Concentration in winners** — after Day 90 data, kill losers, compound into survivors

## Non-Negotiables

- No paid services requiring ongoing labor beyond founder budget sign-off
- Stdlib-first Python tools (per repo CLAUDE.md conventions)
- AI-search resilient niches only — no horizontal/product-based directories
- Image scraping only with outreach-for-permission flow

## Out of Scope (for now)

- Paid ads (SEO-first distribution)
- Mobile apps
- Enterprise sales
- Raising outside capital

## Success Metrics (Phase 0, 90 days, portfolio-aggregate)

- ≥3 of 5 directories indexed with ≥500 cleaned listings
- ≥2 of 5 with ≥1K monthly organic visits by Day 90
- ≥10 inbound lead-form submissions across the portfolio
- ≥1 paying customer on at least 1 property

## Kill Criteria (per property, Day 90)

- <300 monthly visits AND $0 MRR → kill, reallocate
- <$200 MRR AND <1K visits → watchlist, 30-day ultimatum

## Scale Criteria (per property, Day 90)

- ≥$500 MRR OR ≥2K monthly visits → approve Phase 1 scale budget
