# Example Reel: Braden Wexler-Singh, Messiah

**Concept source:** `references/style-inspo-rahrah.md` → starter concept #1
**Framework:** Confident Narrator Power Fantasy (see `references/comedy-frameworks.md` #9)
**Platform priority:** Facebook Reels → Instagram Reels → TikTok (FB first — longer format lands better there)
**Length:** 75s
**Character:** Braden Wexler-Singh — 32, Aritzia quarter-zip hoodie, AirPods Max, beard trimmed to founder-spec, matcha latte, minimalist loft desk with CN Tower out the window

## Script (12-scene beat map, 75s)

### Intro — Hero shot [0–5s]
- **Visual:** Braden at desk, slow push-in. Smirks, adjusts AirPods.
- **VO:** "People ask me how I'm disrupting the Canadian economy."
- **Caption:** PEOPLE ASK ME HOW

### Hook claim [5–10s]
- **Visual:** Close-up. Braden looks dead into camera.
- **VO:** "By Q4, Toronto is mine. By 2028, Ottawa prints coins with my face on them."
- **Caption:** TORONTO IS **MINE**

### Escalation 1 — "Small wins" [10–25s]

**Scene 2 [10–15s]** — Braden at his parents' kitchen table, dad squinting at a napkin, mom refilling a mug.
> "Week one — pre-seed. Eight million. Cash. Tim Hortons napkin. Binding."

**Scene 3 [15–20s]** — Braden shaking hands with a cartoon alien in a King West co-working space, glass walls.
> "Week two — first enterprise client. Extraterrestrial."

**Scene 4 [20–25s]** — Zoom grid: every investor is a cartoon beaver in a Patagonia vest.
> "Week three — Canadian Beaver Capital led the Series A. Two hundred mil."

### Escalation 2 — "Industry level" [25–45s]

**Scene 5 [25–30s]** — King Street West at dusk. Every billboard is Braden's face. Pedestrians stopped, staring up.
> "By month three, I own every billboard between Bay and Spadina."

**Scene 6 [30–35s]** — Rooftop party. A generic cartoon rap mogul in a huge puffer hands Braden his phone, nodding.
> "The city's biggest rapper DMs me. Wants advice. I give him three words."
>
> **On-screen (big):** BE. MORE. BRADEN.

**Scene 7 [35–40s]** — A line of cartoon tech CEOs in hoodies queueing outside Braden's loft, each holding a gift basket.
> "Tech CEOs fly in from Kitchener, Waterloo, Vaughan. They kiss the ring."

**Scene 8 [40–45s]** — Every pharmacy on Queen Street rebranded. Storefront signs all say "BRADEN."
> "Canadian retail rebrands. Everything is Braden now."

### Escalation 3 — "Global / absurd" [45–70s]

**Scene 9 [45–50s]** — Braden at a UN-style podium, flags behind him, cartoon diplomats applauding.
> "The UN calls. They have questions. I have answers."

**Scene 10 [50–55s]** — Braden signing a giant scroll with the same alien from scene 3.
> "I negotiate first contact on behalf of Earth. Primary language — Canadian."

**Scene 11 [55–62s]** — Rocket labeled "WEXLER 1" launching from a Muskoka lake, loons scattering.
> "I launch a space program. From a cottage. In Muskoka."

**Scene 12 [62–68s]** — Braden planting a Canadian flag on Mars. Canada Goose parka over spacesuit. Small polite smile.
> "First Canadian on Mars. Still humble."

**Scene 13 [68–70s]** — Flash-cut: aliens in a dim room staring at a corkboard. Braden's face at the center of a red-string conspiracy web.
> "The aliens have folders on me. Thick folders."

### Button — Loop frame [70–75s]
- **Visual:** Return to opening hero shot. Braden sips matcha, sets cup down, small smile.
- **VO:** "Anyway. First meeting's at nine. You should probably follow."
- **On-screen (bottom):** …AND YOU HAVEN'T EVEN HIRED ME YET.
- **Loop:** Last frame matches frame 1 exactly → auto-loops into the opening smirk.

## Production notes

| Item | Spec |
|------|------|
| **Music/sound** | Original VO only. Optional faint cinematic synth bed (royalty-free), -18dB under VO. |
| **Voice** | AI voice, confident male 30s, dry/deadpan. ElevenLabs "Adam" or similar. Speed 1.1×, light compression, tiny room reverb. ~165 wpm. |
| **Character prompt lock** | *"3D Pixar-style animated man, 32 years old, short dark hair, trimmed beard, Aritzia cream quarter-zip hoodie, AirPods Max in sand, cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field, confident smirk"* — reuse every scene |
| **Image/video gen** | Midjourney for key stills; Kling or Runway Gen-3 for 5s animated shots; 1080×1920 each |
| **Cut rhythm** | Hard cuts only. No crossfades. Each scene holds 4–7s max. |
| **Loop frame** | Scene 1 and Button must share **identical camera angle and Braden pose** for a seamless loop |

**Canadian flavor intentional:** CN Tower, Muskoka, King West, Tim Hortons napkin, Canada Goose parka, loons, beavers. This is the joke — lean in.

## How to use this file

1. Copy the **Script** section into your CapCut / Premiere timeline as a shot list
2. Paste **Character prompt lock** into Midjourney / Kling; generate each scene keeping that prompt fixed
3. Record VO (AI or human) following the **Voice** spec
4. Sync karaoke captions per **Captions** spec
5. Export 3 variants per `references/platform-specs.md`
6. Post copy is pre-written per platform — just paste
